**Result:** hold — best approach (v3_dual) at 262 us/call, no default flip
**Root cause of floor:** Metal cmd-buf create→commit→wait = ~250us regardless of GPU kernel time (~15us). Kernel optimization cannot break this without TCB batching.

**Approach 1 (geometry tuning):**
- v3_8r: 264 us (5.1% vs simdmat), parity PASS
- v3_dual: 262 us (5.7% vs simdmat), parity PASS
- Outcome: hold (200-300us), kernels committed as opt-in

**Approach 2 (simdgroup_matrix 8×8):**
- gemm_q4_k_m_fused_simd (existing kernel): 2063 us — 8× slower due to 256 barriers/block
- Outcome: failed

**Approach 3 (llama.cpp faithful port, N_R0=4, sumy trick):**
- v3_llama: 1698 us in loaded system — slower than v3_dual due to 64 extra simd_sum calls
- Self-heal applied: fixed sumy subtraction order (must subtract after simd_sum, not before)
- Parity PASS at max_abs_diff=9.16e-5
- Outcome: failed (worse than Approach 1)

**Gate decision:** 262 us → "hold" → gemm_q4_k_schedule stays "v2"
