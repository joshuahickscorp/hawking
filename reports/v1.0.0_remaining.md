## New Kernels (opt-in)
- **gemm_q4_k_m_simdmat** (`schedule="simdmat"`): 128-thread TG, 4 rows/TG, paired nibble reads.
  Enable via `profile.selected.gemm_q4_k_schedule = "simdmat"`.
  At rows=1408, cols=2048: 299µs vs 524µs v2 (1.75× synthetic). Production extrapolation: ~199µs.

- **flash_attn_decode_kernel** (`attn_block_schedule="flash"`): Online softmax, FLASH_TG=128.
  TG shmem: ~4.5KB vs ~20KB (seq_len-proportional). ~6× better occupancy on Apple M2.
  Enable via `profile.selected.attn_block_schedule = "flash"`.
  Parity: max_diff=1.07e-6 vs mla_decode_kernel across 4 shapes.
