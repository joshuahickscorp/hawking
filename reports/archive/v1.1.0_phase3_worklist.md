## Skipped kernels (rationale)

- `mla_decode_and_o_proj_arena_tcb`: 7+9 set_bytes, complex attention kernel — Sonnet only
- `encode_batched_gemv_indexed_tcb` / `encode_batched_gemv_fused_gu_tcb`: contain u64 args
  (`gate_offset`, `up_offset` as u64); Metal struct packing for u64 requires care
- Non-TCB functions (e.g., `gemv_q4_k_m_simd`, `rmsnorm_metal_buf`): not in the hot TCB path;
  converting them buys nothing until ICB uses them
