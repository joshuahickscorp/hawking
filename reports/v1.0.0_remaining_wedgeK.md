## Iteration log

### Iteration 1 (initial simdmat, 8-rows/TG, 256 threads):
- Scale pre-load (s_byte[8], m_byte[8]) extracted once per block
- xl[8] activation preload into registers
- Paired nibble reads (k0/k1 share one byte → 4 reads/thread/block instead of 8)
- Result: rows=1408,cols=2048 — simdmat=302µs vs v2=537µs (1.78×)
- STUCK status: 302µs > 300µs (barely above STUCK threshold)

### Iteration 2 (4-rows/TG, 128 threads — doubles TG count):
- Changed geometry: 4 simdgroups × 32 = 128 threads per TG, 4 rows per TG
- Grid: ceil(rows/4) × 128 (vs iter1's ceil(rows/8) × 256)
- Doubles TG count for rows=1408: 352 TGs vs 176 TGs — better GPU parallelism
- New dedicated dispatcher `dispatch_q4_k_m_simdmat_pinned`
- Result: rows=1408,cols=2048 — simdmat=299µs vs v2=524µs (1.75×)
- STUCK status: 299µs < 300µs ✓

## Structural Notes

**Kernel `gemm_q4_k_m_simdmat` (`shaders/quant.metal`):**
- 4 simdgroups (128 threads) per TG; 1 row per simdgroup; 4 rows per TG
- Grid: (ceil(rows/4)*128, 1, 1)
- Pre-loads: sb[8], mb[8] (sub-block scales), xl[8] (activations) before k-loop
- Paired nibble loop: 4 iterations × 2 k-values per byte = 4 device reads vs 8
- simd_sum reduction, zero barriers
- cols must be a multiple of 256 (Q4_K block constraint)

**Dispatcher `dispatch_q4_k_m_simdmat_pinned` (`kernels/mod.rs`):**
- Separate from `dispatch_q4_k_m_gemv_v2_pinned` — different TG geometry
- Buffer layout: buf0=w_q4 (offset), buf1=x, buf2=y, bytes3=rows, bytes4=cols
- No shmem

**Routing (`deepseek_v2.rs::moe_expert_matmul_dispatch`):**
- `gemm_q4_k_schedule="simdmat"` → `gemv_q4_k_m_simdmat_pinned`
- Falls through to scalar path if model_buf not available (same as v2)

**Parity test `tests/v1k_q4kgemm_simdmat_parity.rs`:** 3 tests at atol=1e-3.
