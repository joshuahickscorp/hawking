## 🪦 Dead / held (do not re-spawn — see reports/dead_levers.md)

- **Host-side per-dispatch overhead family** — ICB, concurrent QKV encoder
  (+1.68%), PSO-transition batching (1.06×), CPU-encode (0.51%). The 36 ms/token
  "gap" is **real GPU-side**, not host. Kernel BW efficiency is the lever.
- **W4A8 / mixed-precision as default** — quality 20% bit-identical (N=100),
  perf 1.115× (below 1.20× gate), sub-additive with predec. Held behind a flag.
- **Predec `_4r`** — speculative, motivated by a rejected trace; unvalidated,
  opt-in only. Gated on 0.1 profiling.
- **FFN block-256 sparsity**, **MLA Phase 4**, **Eagle5 v1 routing-mask**,
  **f16 residual**, **MoE megakernel/serial dispatch**, **Q5_0 simd_shuffle**,
  **LM-head simdmat as a tps lever** — all with killing evidence in the registry.
